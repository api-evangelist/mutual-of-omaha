Mutual of Omaha Retirement Services is hiring in response to expected sales of 401(k) Admin Advantage SM, an ERISA section 3(16) administrative support and fiduciary service option for Mutual of Omaha's 401(k) plans. Since its launch in June, approximately 50 percent of proposals and 401(k) sales have included the 401(k) Admin Advantage SM option.
